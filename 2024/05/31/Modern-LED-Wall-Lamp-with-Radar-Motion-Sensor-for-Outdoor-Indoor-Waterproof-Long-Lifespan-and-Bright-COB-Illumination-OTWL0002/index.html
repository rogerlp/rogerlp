Modern LED Wall Lamp with Radar Motion Sensor for Outdoor & Indoor Waterproof & Long Lifespan and Bright COB Illumination OTWL0002

Posted on 2024-05-31 Edited on 2024-06-06 In Outdoor Light

Key Features

  • The product is comprised of high-quality black aluminum alloy and white PC shell that is resistant to oxidation and fading. Its modern and simple design offers an elegant appearance.
  • The light can be installed vertically on walls using screws and expansion pillar hinges.
  • It emits no UV or IR radiation, has low power consumption, and a lifespan of up to 50,000 hours.
  • The LED COB light provides bright illumination on walls while protecting your eyesight. Soft lighting creates a relaxing atmosphere that uplifts your mood.
  • This product is suitable for both indoor and outdoor use. It has an IP65 waterproof rating, making it ideal for homes, bars, cafes, hallways, balconies, stairs, walkways, gardens, and outdoor decor.

Specifications

  • This is a specification for an outdoor LED wall lamp with a radar motion sensor and intelligent light control.
  • The lamp is constructed with high-quality die-cast aluminum material for efficient heat dissipation and has a power rating of 9W, operating on AC85-265V voltage.
  • Its body color is black, and it is designed to be waterproof with an IP65 rating.
  • The radar motion sensor feature enables the light to only work at night when someone walks into its 3-6 meter range.
  • The light turns off automatically after 30 seconds when there is no one around.
  • For customers who prefer a non-sensor option, they will not have access to the sensing and intelligent light control function.

Notes

  1. When installing lamps, you must cut off the power first.
  2. Professional technician installation.
  3. The installation parts of each part of the lamp installation must be installed in place.
  4. The lamps must be regularly maintained by professional technicians.
