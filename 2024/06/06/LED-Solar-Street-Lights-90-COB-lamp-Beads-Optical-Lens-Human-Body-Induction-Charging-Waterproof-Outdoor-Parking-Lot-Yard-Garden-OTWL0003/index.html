Three Lighting Modes:

  • Mode 1: Human Induction + Light Control: During the nighttime or in dark areas, the light will automatically turn on when it senses human movement. After a delay of 20-25 seconds, it will turn off when no movement is detected.
  • Mode 2: Dim Light + Human Induction + Light Control: At night, the light will automatically turn on with dim light. When motion is detected within 3-5 meters, it will switch to bright light for 20-25 seconds before returning to dim light.
  • Mode 3: Light Control: The light will automatically turn on during the nighttime or in dark areas and turn off during the daytime or in bright areas based on its built-in light sensor.

Key Features

  • Our solar street light utilizes a solar charging design and can be controlled via remote control, automatically turning on when it detects darkness. Charging time is dependent on the intensity of sunlight.
  • Featuring a human body infrared sensor with a sensing distance of 10 meters, it automatically illuminates when movement is detected, providing safety in dark environments.
  • With easy installation that requires no wiring, this waterproof wall lamp can be installed outdoors and at multiple angles, making it suitable for doorways, courtyards, aisles, workshops, and more. It also comes equipped with a remote control that allows you to turn the product off or cover it when not in use.
